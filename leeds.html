Leeds is a very tiny hamlet with a couple of bakeries/cafes, two bars and two restaurants. Located two miles from the I-87. One of the best swimming holes of the area, marble rocks, is located in Leeds. Our location is very close this charming little town. One of the most beautiful small towns of New York, Hudson is just 14 minutes away and so is the Hudson Amtrak station. The Windham ski resort is 27 minutes and Hunter ski resort is 36 minutes away.
