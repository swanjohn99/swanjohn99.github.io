Palenville, USA's original art colony, is a tiny hamlet at the start of the scenic route to Hunter mountain. It is connected to New York by a Trailways bus. Walk to Cafes, Grocery, Restaurants, Hiking Trails and Swimming holes or borrow our bikes to explore farther. If you brought a car, turn off the GPS and embark on a journey of discovery through random little country roads to rolling hills, meadows, farms and the beautiful little towns nearby. Stargazer tiny house

Two tiny houses for the price of one -the stargazer and the pool house, both are connected by a private deck. The pool house is heated and has a bathroom and kitchenette. It is half an hour or less from Woodstock, Hunter, Hudson, Catskill, Saugetries and beautiful hikes around Kaaterskill.
